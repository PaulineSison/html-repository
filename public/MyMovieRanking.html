The Best Movies According To Me

My top 3 movies of all time:

Memoir of a Snail

This is one my favorite movies because not only it is entertaining, but it somehow depicts life's balance of bitter-sweet moments that surely many audiences will relate to.

The Little Prince

This movie also earns a spot in my favorites because it makes you wonder about life and all of the lessons that you get along the way as you go through it.

Amelie

I love this movie because it is very eccentric, artsy, and comedic. Very entertaining! It has a bit of everything into it. It is very fun to watch!
